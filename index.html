Biography

Yu Guan is a post-doctoral fellow in the ICTEAM institute at UCLouvain working with Prof. Pierre-Antoine Absil. Her research interests include tensor decomposition, tensor completion and machine learning. She got her PHD degree at National University of Singapore under the supervisor Prof. Delin Chu. She had a visiting exprience working with Prof. Moody T. Chu.
